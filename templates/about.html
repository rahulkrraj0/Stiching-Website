The Process

1

Studio Consultation

Visit our studio for a personal consultation. We take precise measurements, discuss your style preferences, and answer all your questions.

2

Fabric Selection & Pricing

Choose from our library of premium fabrics (silk, linen, organic cotton, hand-embroidered textiles) or bring your own. Pricing is confirmed based on your selections and finishing details.

3

Custom Pattern & Toile Fitting

I draft a custom pattern based on your measurements and fit a trial toile (muslin sample) for structured designs. We refine the fit until it's perfect.

4

Hand-Finished Creation

Your dress is handcrafted with precision. Hems are finished by hand by local artisans. The result is a garment you'll wear for years.

5

Fittings & Collection

Your finished garment is collected or fitted at our studio, with every detail reviewed together before final collection.
